Welcome to Holland Foot and Ankle

At Holland Foot & Ankle Center, we are dedicated to providing the highest quality professional foot care so that our patients may enjoy the highest quality of life. Since 1984, the Holland Foot and Ankle Center Podiatric Physicians have dedicated themselves to you - the patient - your needs, your concerns, and your feet. Our four certified podiatrists, combined with a team of health-care support staff, guarantee friendly, professional, and complete podiatric treatment for you and your family.

Our Mission . . . Our Pledge

As podiatric physicians, our pledge is to deliver high quality, professional medical treatment in a caring and compassionate environment.

Our Services

We provide treatment for a variety of foot conditions, ranging from minor problems such as ingrown toenails or warts to complex problems, such as painful flat feet requiring reconstructive surgery. Minor procedures can usually be performed in our office. Our normal standard of care is to use the most conservative measures that we believe will solve the problem at hand. If the condition is not then resolved, we progress to more aggressive treatment. Surgery is indicated for some, but not all, foot problems.

Our state-of-the-art Foot and Ankle Centers offer many alternatives in treatment such as laser, endoscopy/arthroscopy, office-based and hospital surgery.
